\section*{Algorithm}
CG-CEL: When $E > \frac{S}{2}$, first allocate $\frac{c_i}{2}$, then use CEL for remainder: find $\mu$ such that $\sum \max(0, \frac{c_i}{2} - \mu) = E - \frac{S}{2}$

\begin{align*}
S &= 100, \quad \frac{S}{2} = 50, \quad E - \frac{S}{2} = 5 \\
\text{First:} \quad &[12.5, 12.5, 12.5, 12.5] \\
4(12.5-\mu) &= 5 \\
50 - 4\mu &= 5 \\
\mu &= 11.25 \\
x_i &= 12.5 + (12.5 - 11.25) = 12.5 + 1.25 = \boxed{13.75}
\end{align*}
